Portnotes: An excellent way to take notes.
Designed for students.

Portnotes is the new way to keep track of notes. It's loaded with convenient features and templates that help students keep an organized and clean look paper free.Visit their website to become a member.

Our Mission

Being college students can be very stressful. We decided to make an application that takes a little stress out of the day. We designed an aesthetically pleasing and simple way to access all your notes in an organized dashboard. You can view all your notebooks and look at all your notes with a click of a button. A great variety of templates, such as cornell notes and graphing paper, are available to choose from. The best part is the sleek design, and no paper!
